# Meeting Plan — Outreach Coordinators

## Recommended Meeting Venue

### Greater Columbus Convention Center
- **Address:** 400 North High Street, Downtown Columbus, OH 43215
- **Why Columbus, OH:** It is the nearest major city to the geographic midpoint of Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta, and is well-connected by air and highway to all three cities.
- **Venue:** Greater Columbus Convention Center (conference_centre, importance score 0.349)
- **Neighborhood:** Arena District / Uptown District, Downtown Columbus

## Distance Analysis

| From → To | Approx. Distance |

| Chicago → Columbus | ~500 mi |

| **Total round-trip** | **~1,710 mi** |

## Access

- **ColumBUS (public transit):** Routes serve downtown and the convention center
- **Airports:** John Glenn Columbus International Airport (CMH) — direct flights from all three cities
- **Parking:** Several garages within walking distance of the convention center
- **Hotels:** Multiple options within 0.5 mi (Hilton, Hyatt, Marriott, Drury Inn)
